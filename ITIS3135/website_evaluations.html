Website Evaluations

Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc.

A leading supplier of X-Ray Diffraction and Fluorescence equipment, offering a wide range of products and services to research and industrial clients.

  • Contrast: The site uses a minimal color scheme to ensure text and elements are easily distinguishable.
  • Repetition: The layout consistently uses fonts and color themes across pages.
  • Alignment: Content is well-organized, aligning to a grid that makes the information easy to follow.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped together, improving the overall readability and navigation of the site.

Aprilia USA

Offers information on the latest motorcycles, racing events, and dealer locations. Known for high-performance motorcycles and a rich racing heritage.

  • Contrast: High contrast between background and text for readability, with vibrant images to attract attention.
  • Repetition: The site maintains a consistent visual theme across all pages, reinforcing the brand identity.
  • Alignment: The layout is clean, with a strong visual hierarchy that guides the user's eye through the content.
  • Proximity: Content is logically organized, with clear separations between different sections and categories.
